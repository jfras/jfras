About me

I am PhD student at Queen Mary University of London, School of Engineering and Materials Science. I am member of Advanced Robotics at Queen Mary group (ARQ) led by prof. Kaspar Althoefer. My research focuses on soft robotics, in particular on soft actuation.
Alongside I am an engineer in Industrial Research Institute for Automation and Measurements PIAP where I work not only on soft hardware but also on autonomous behaviours, vision and human perception .
