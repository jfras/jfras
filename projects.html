Projects

Soft pneumatic octopus

Swimming octopus robot. No rigid parts. Currently pneumatic with external power source, but I work extensively to make it enclosed hydraulic device. Robot can swim straight, turn in any direction and twist along its primary axis.


Octopus' tentacle

The robot is driven by novel pneumatic actuator. It maximizes the actuation area while providing 2 degrees of freedom.



Soft prosthetic hand

Prosthetic hand made with soft materials only. It consist of exoskeleton made of less flexible silicone rubber that defines its motion and soft fluidical actuators housed in fingers. It provides 6 degrees of actuation as the thumb contains 2 separate sets of actuators that allow to bend and to reconfigure its orientation.



Soft industrial grippers

Pneumatic grippers for special tasks I developed for PIAP. They are soft and compliant so suitable for fragile and delicate objects. They do not require any sophisticated control as they adapt to the handled objects. It has been design for mobile antiterrorist robots manufactured at PIAP.
