About me

I pursued a PhD degree at Queen Mary University of London, School of Engineering and Materials Science. I was a member of the Advanced Robotics at Queen Mary group (ARQ), led by Prof. Kaspar Althoefer. My research focused on soft robotics, particularly soft actuation.
Currently, I am also a Robotics Research Engineer at Ocado Technology, where I work on developing novel and groundbreaking robotic solutions.
